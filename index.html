About Us

Intro to Technology Sciences Club

Technology sciences club is a hub where the best students of New World Private School meets to discuss and implement the lastest inventions that going to play a huge role in the worlds economy and the future concepts that envolves in shaping our lifes

Our team works to develop four main skills
  • Search skills (problem solving).
  • Graphic Design.
  • Video Editing.
  • Programming/Coding.

Search (Problem solving) Team

This team is working on improving the problem solving skill wich is the first block of the success and the foundation of leading teams and businesses.

We in Technology sciences club are concentrating on searching skills which is a big part of problem solving to enable our members to take the most of the available resources by learning how to find the right information in the minimum time.

Programming (Coding) Team

This team works on PROGRAMMING the essence of the technology to widen our horizon by bringing all of our ideas to life with a little ( portion of MAGIC ) piece of code.
In addition to coding the programming journey includes alot of valuable skills such as :
  • Creative thinking.
  • Algorithms.
  • Patience

Graphic Desing Team

Graphic Desing is a necessary skill in the future world where you have only a few seconds to grab the audience attention so the first impression is sometimes more valuable than the real content and that is what we work on in this team.

Video Editing Team

What's better than freezing memories and sharing experinces?

But video editing reach beyond that and include marketing campaigns, business plans and infographics and so much more so this teams' work is to learn the science of video editing and master its tools.
